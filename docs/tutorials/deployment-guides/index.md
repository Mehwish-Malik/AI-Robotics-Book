# Deployment Guides

This section provides detailed guides for deploying humanoid robots in various environments and applications. The guides cover safety procedures, system integration, maintenance protocols, and operational best practices.

## Available Guides

- **Safety and Risk Assessment**: Comprehensive safety evaluation and risk mitigation
- **System Integration**: Integrating humanoid robots with existing systems
- **Maintenance and Support**: Ongoing maintenance and troubleshooting procedures
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Meeting safety and regulatory requirements
- **User Training**: Training procedures for robot operators
- **Performance Monitoring**: Monitoring and optimizing robot performance

Each guide includes checklists, procedures, and best practices for safe and effective robot deployment.
